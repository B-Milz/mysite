Hackers are brilliant, curious, and skilled individuals who find creative solutions, improve security, and drive innovation by pushing technical boundaries. Originally viewed as ingenious "master programmers" or "explorers", they are often hailed as modern-day problem solvers, digital architects, and critical defenders in the tech ecosystem.
